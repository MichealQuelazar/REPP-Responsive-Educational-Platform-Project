About REEP

Transforming rural education through innovative technology, community collaboration, and unwavering commitment to educational equality.

Our Mission

To bridge the educational gap in rural India by providing accessible, high-quality learning experiences in local languages, empowering students and educators alike.

Our Vision

A future where every child in rural India has access to world-class education and the opportunity to succeed.

Our Impact

Serving over 10 lakh students and 1 lakh teachers across 28 states and union territories, REEP is making quality education a reality in rural India.

Our Journey

  • 2020: REEP founded to address the digital divide in rural India.
  • 2021: Launched first digital classroom pilot in Uttar Pradesh and Maharashtra.
  • 2022: Partnered with local NGOs to expand to 10 states.
  • 2023: Reached 1 lakh students and 5,000 teachers with free resources.
  • 2024: Introduced AI-powered learning and expanded to all 28 states and UTs.
